so who are the customers?
we have a few basic groups
  • Green: Developers or people who want to do advanced modeling/programming with the data
  • Lime: AFF users who want to access tables and mainly volume data, and potentially build something or incorporate a layer into their own tool
  • Yellow: Yellow - AFF users who want to download volume data and then run stats and/or manipulate data
  • Orange: Orange - Show me the #s on the site or let me download just the data I need but I'm not that technical
  • Blue: Small scale, informal data consumer, not familiar with Census data holdings and just wants to see raw number on the site
  1. How are different user types currently searching for and interacting with data?
  2. What defines the “right” result format and content for different user types?
  3. How can we help folks discover Census data?
